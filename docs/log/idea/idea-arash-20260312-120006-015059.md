# idea

## Overview
Feature detection and characterization for pathological ECOG patterns

Brainstorm: Develop diagnostic measures for two classes of signal contamination in ECoG recordings:

1. **Spatial discontinuity patterns** — Striped high-power artifacts (checkerboard or column-wise anomalies, non-physiological). Proposed metrics: spatial frequency power ratio, spatial SNR.

2. **Transient line noise bouts** — Unknown frequency components appearing episodically (beyond standard 60/120/180 Hz harmonics). TTL artifacts well-characterized (40 Hz carrier + 10 Hz envelope) contaminate column 10 on 16×16 grids; separate trivial trimming from true noise detection.

**Measurement approach:** Time-frequency spectrograms with spatial decomposition to detect local maxima in frequency and space jointly. Leverage existing max-blob detection in `cogpy.core.detect` or explore simpler alternatives.

**Questions for exploration:** How to detect line noise across unknown frequencies without a priori assumptions? Can we correlate spatial patterns with noise bout timing? Should we decompose detection by axis (AP vs. ML)?
